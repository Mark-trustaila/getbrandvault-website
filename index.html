How it works

Connect, monitor, act, report

BrandVault connects to your registries and existing systems. The AI agent handles the routine work. You stay in control.

Connect
BrandVault connects directly to trademark registries worldwide via LawPanel's live data API, pulling current status, events, and deadlines automatically. Also integrates with existing case management and billing systems via API. Setup in hours, not weeks.
Monitor
Every mark in your portfolio is monitored continuously. Renewal deadlines, office actions, status changes, and registry correspondence are captured automatically and matched to the correct matter. Nothing falls through.
Act
The BrandVault AI agent completes renewal, reclassification, and filing forms automatically from your portfolio data. Where registries support API submission — including UKIPO — BrandVault files directly. No email, no PDFs, no manual re-keying. You review and approve before anything is submitted.
Report
Clients and stakeholders get real-time portfolio visibility through a shared access portal. Self-serve reporting by mark, registry, jurisdiction, or deadline — without involving your team. Time tracking and billing integrated or connected to your existing system.

FAQ

Questions & answers

How does BrandVault get its registry data? +

BrandVault connects directly to trademark registries via the LawPanel Firms API, pulling live status, event, and deadline data automatically. There are no manual imports and no periodic batch syncs — data is current at all times across UKIPO, EUIPO, WIPO, USPTO and 180+ other jurisdictions.

Who is BrandVault built for? +

In-house IP teams at brand owners who want direct visibility of their portfolio without relying on agents for every update — and IP boutique firms managing multiple client portfolios who need to scale without scaling headcount. BrandVault gives both the same live data and AI automation with access controls appropriate to each role.

What does "automated filing" mean in practice? +

BrandVault completes filing forms automatically using data already in your portfolio — applicant details, mark information, class specifications — removing the need to re-key information you already have. Where registries support API submission (including UKIPO as it rolls out digital filing), BrandVault submits and pays directly. For registries still requiring manual submission, the completed form is prepared for review and download. Nothing is submitted without your approval.

Can BrandVault replace our existing case management system? +

BrandVault includes lightweight case management for opposition, cancellation, and contentious matters. For firms with an existing system, BrandVault connects via API so portfolio data, deadlines, and AI-prepared documents flow into your current workflow rather than requiring a system replacement.

How does the client access portal work? +

Clients get a live view of their portfolio — marks, statuses, upcoming deadlines, and renewal history — without needing to contact your team. They can run their own reports filtered by mark, class, registry, or jurisdiction. Access is read-only by default and fully controlled by your team.

How does migration from an existing system work? +

BrandVault accepts portfolio imports in standard formats. Because live registry data is pulled directly, much of the portfolio can be reconstructed automatically — reducing the migration burden substantially compared with moving between legacy systems. We support migration as part of onboarding.
